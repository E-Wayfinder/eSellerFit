# Market Pressure Calculator

   A professional calculator to assess competitive market pressure for e-commerce sellers.

   ## How to use

   1. Open `market-pressure.html` in your browser
   2. Enter your email and product category
   3. Answer 12 questions about your market
   4. Get your Market Pressure Index and strategic guidance

   ## Features

   - Gauge-style dial visualization
   - 4 competitive forces analysis (Demand, Supply, Advertising, Margin)
   - Ocean classification (Blue, Amber, Red)
   - Dominant force identification
   - 3 CTA options (Free call, $449 dossier, $549 full intake)

   ## Technology

   - Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript
   - No dependencies
   - Responsive design
   - Mobile friendly

   ## Files

   - `market-pressure.html` - Main calculator page
   - `assets/css/shared-ui.css` - Styling
   - `assets/js/calculator-shell.js` - Shared utilities
   - `assets/js/config.js` - Configuration
   - `assets/js/market-pressure-data.js` - 12 questions
   - `assets/js/market-pressure-engine.js` - Scoring logic
   - `assets/js/market-pressure-render.js` - Results display
